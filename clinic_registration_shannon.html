SHANNON PETERS CLINIC: July 10th and 11th, 2010

Shannon Peters

We are pleased to welcome Shannon Peters of SPeters Dressage to Traumhof for a clinic on July 10th and 11th. All riding slots are full, but we are assembling a waitlist. We have plenty of space for auditors and it should be an excellent learning opportunity to watch Shannon teach some of the best riders in the Pacific Northwest! We will be charging $25 per day for auditing.

Please join us for this educational and fun weekend! Having Shannon up here is going to be a rare treat, as she does a limited number of clinics.

We will have a BBQ party on the patio Saturday evening, all welcome, please RSVP on the auditing registration form so we have enough food and wine.

We will once again be offering delicious box lunches from The Grange Cafe. They are $15 each and include a drink and dessert. They will be made and delivered each day, so must be ordered and paid for in advance. You can choose from regular or vegetarian options, or even specify special dietary needs - The Grange is very good at providing custom meals.
