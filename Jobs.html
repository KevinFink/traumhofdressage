Traumhof Jobs

Traumhof is looking for additional barn help at our dressage boarding and training facility between Carnation and Duvall. Extensive horse experience and reliable references mandatory!

The job includes feeding the horses hay and grain, keeping their water buckets clean and full, turnout, and blanketing. Other duties may include but are not limited to watering and raking arenas, keeping everything clean (sweeping, dusting, emptying garbage cans, etc), and handwalking horses. (No stall cleaning required.) Attention to detail mandatory, as we offer top care to our clients - both 2 and 4 legged. Need an educated eye for any problems with the horses - legs, colic, colds, changes in eating habits or other behaviors, etc.

The schedule varies (hours of day and days of week) with seasons.

Looking for long-term employees who want to be part of a happy, successful team. Must work very well with co-workers, horses, and clients, be willing to learn, and always be upbeat and positive.

Must be a U.S. citizen or possess a valid resident/work permit and maintain a current Washington State driver's license. Must live within 20-30 minutes of farm.

Note that this is not a riding or training position.

Contact Jessica Lyman at jessica@traumhofdressage.com to apply.
