Help Wanted

Traumhof is looking for part-time barn help at our dressage boarding and training facility between Carnation and Duvall. Extensive horse experience and reliable references mandatory!

The job includes feeding hay and grain, watering, turnout, and blanketing. Other duties may include but are not limited to watering and raking arenas, sweeping, dusting, emptying garbage cans, keeping everything cleaning, minor facility repairs (fences, etc), and generally keeping the farm beautiful! Attention to detail mandatory, as we offer top care to our clients - both 2 and 4 legged. Need an educated eye for any problems with the horses - legs, colic, colds, changes in eating habits or other behaviors, etc.

The schedule varies (hours of day and days of week) with seasons.

Looking for long-term employees who want to be part of a happy, successful team. Must work very well with co-workers, horses, and clients, take correction well, and always be upbeat and positive.

Must be a U.S. citizen or possess a valid resident/work permit and maintain a current Washington State driver's license. Must live within 20-30 minutes of farm.

Please send your resume to Jessica Lyman <jessica@traumhofdressage.com>.
