TheraPlate

Several years ago (2011) we purchased a TheraPlate to help the rehab horses at Traumhof maintain muscle tone and bone density and to help them recover from various injuries. We have been very pleased with the results, not only for these purposes but even more so in other areas. For example, it has helped us resolve many mild colics (and some that appeared quite serious) with little or no vet intervention. It has also helped reduce abcess recurrences in horses with a long history of chronic abcesses, healed hoof granulation where nothing else has helped, and accelerated hoof growth in multiple cases.

Vibration therapy has a well-documented track record, with hundreds of peer-reviewed studies, for helping both humans and animals recover from injuries as well as develop and maintain healthy bodies. It's used in many of the top equine training programs across many disciplines.

Whether the horses recognize the benefits or just like how it makes them feel, it's clear that they enjoy using it - they eagerly rush to it, step up onto the plate, and stand happily for the duration of their (typically 20 minute) session. We (and many of our clients) also enjoy using the TheraPlate ourselves, both to help relax sore muscles and to promote better fitness and health.

In fact, we have been so impressed with it we recently signed up to help distribute it! Because we, and the manufacturer, believe so strongly in its benefits, and want as many people (and more importantly, horses) as possible to have access to it, we are offering a free two-week trial at your facility. The unit will be shipped directly to you and we will help you set it up (in person if you're in the Seattle area, on the phone otherwise). In the unlikely event you decide it's not right for you, we'll have it picked up, all at no cost to you.

The equine version (two units that interconnect to create a 3'x7' platform) costs $6000 (plus shipping and handling). A variety of financing options are available, including an interest-free 12 month payment schedule (on approved credit).
