TheraPlate

Vibration therapy has a well-documented track record (the TheraPlate website cites over 200 peer-reviewed studies) for helping both humans and animals recover from injuries as well as develop and maintain healthy bodies. It's used in many of the top equine training programs across many disciplines. We have been so impressed with the TheraPlate for Equine Vibration Therapy that we recently signed up to help distribute it.

Whether the horses recognize the benefits or just like how it makes them feel, it's clear that they enjoy using it - they eagerly rush to it and step up onto the plate. Danny, who has a long history of hoof complaints, is a particularly big fan, and it appears to be really helping reduce the granulation on his hooves. We (and many of our clients) have also enjoyed using the TheraPlate ourselves, both to help relax sore muscles and to promote better fitness and health.

In addition to its proven benefits, we have observed that it helps relieve mild colics - the first thing we do upon noticing a horse in distress is step them up on the Theraplate, and more often than not see (and hear and smell) fast relief. You never know for sure what would have happened with just hand-walking, or without doing anything at all, but based on our experience over the years, it makes a big difference.
