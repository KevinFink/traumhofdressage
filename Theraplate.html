TheraPlate

Elf on TheraPlate at Traumhof

Several years ago (2011) we purchased a TheraPlate to help the rehab horses at Traumhof maintain muscle tone and bone density and to help them recover from various injuries. We have been very pleased with the results, not only for these purposes but even more so in other areas. For example, it has helped us resolve many mild colics (and some that appeared quite serious) with little or no vet intervention. It has also helped reduce abcess recurrences in horses with a long history of chronic abcesses, healed hoof granulation where nothing else has helped, and accelerated hoof growth in multiple cases.

Vibration therapy (and more specifically Whole Body Vibration, or WBV) has a well-documented track record, with hundreds of peer-reviewed studies, for helping both humans and animals recover from injuries as well as develop and maintain healthy bodies. It's used in many of the top equine training programs across many disciplines. We particularly like TheraPlate's 4 Zone Vortex Wave design for its zero impact experience. Under the rubber top there are four separate metal plates, one for each of the horse's legs. Each plate is driven in a horizontal circular motion by a separate motor. The controller allows you to vary the frequency of this motion from 0 up to 100 Hz (cycles per second). These fast, tiny circles vibrate the entire body, but there is no compression or rocking, so no risk of further injury or aggravating existing conditions.

Whether the horses recognize the benefits or just like how it makes them feel, it's clear that they enjoy using our TheraPlate - they eagerly rush to it, step up onto the plate, and stand happily for the duration of their (typically 20 minute) session. We (and many of our clients) also enjoy using the TheraPlate ourselves, both to help relax sore muscles and to promote better fitness and health.
