We are excited to announce that we are now distributing the wonderful Theraplate Revolution therapeutic whole body vibration platform. We've had such great results with ours (for both the horses and people) that we decided to help distribute them.
Check out our fan page on Facebook for the latest news and tons of pictures of the facility, horses, clinics, etc.

We offer a unique training opportunity for our clients. Our resident trainer, Nicki Grandia, has ridden and trained extensively with Shannon and Steffen Peters, among many other excellent trainers. Her lessons and training program reflects the "Team Peters" approach, emphasizing high expectations for correct riding, softness with the horses, and customization to the conformation and temperament of the horse.

In addition, we regularly host clinics with Shannon, Steffen, and Lientje. This gives our clients the opportunity to learn from some of the best trainers in the world, with their own perspectives and strengths, while maintaining the same overall philosophy. Nicki is a great trainer in her own right, and adding in the amazing experience and skills of the S. Peters Team provides a unique and amazing training program unequalled in the Pacific Northwest.
