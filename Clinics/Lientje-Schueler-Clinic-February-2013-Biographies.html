Horse & Rider Biographies for Lientje Schueler Clinic, February 2013

Danielle Tihon is riding Adagio, a 9 yo Swedish Warmblood gelding by Amiral out of Deja Vu. They are training third level.

Nicki Grandia is riding Unycus, an 11 yo Dutch Warmblood stallion by Grafiet out of Octrola, owned by Linda Smyth. “Uno” just switched to dressage last year, but has already had great success at 3rd & 4th level over the summer show season. Nicki is a USDF Bronze, Silver, and Gold Medalist trainer here at Traumhof.

Nicki is also riding Rudeau, Elisienne's 9 year old son by Ruffian (x Rubenstein).

Paige Ruhl is riding Yahtzee, an (almost) 6 year old Friesian gelding owned by Cathy Petrie. Yahtzee is schooling Second level, and will be showing First and probably Second this year. And he is adorable.

Laurie Summers is riding Rosalyn (Roz), a Hanovarian mare out of Regazzoni (out of Rubinstein I). She is coming 8 this spring. From Laurie: "I purchased her this past September in Kelowna BC where she had been in training for 1 year with a trainer who had her as a sales project for a client who was unable to ride her. She is a very forward thinking mare with 3 quality gaits and a "can do," attitude. We are working on basics at this point as we know she has all the bells and whistles but needs some time with learning to just listen to her rider and be patient. She has very little experience going beyond her own back yard and has never been shown. We are hoping to get her to a place where she can rest and relax and enjoy the moment. She is very smart and fun to work with and is quite a worker bee. I look forward to being able to bring her to Traumhof for her first clinic."

Jill Cheevers is riding Calvin (Cool Diamond), a 6 year old KWPN gelding by Lingh. He has not yet been shown. From Jill: "I've had him for 1.75 years. He is sensitive and athletic so while the work is not difficult for him, his ability to stay with me mentally is more the challange. Calvin has played with movements through 3rd level including green tempi changes and baby pirouettes. My goal for the clinic is to work on his connection and to see if he can hold it together mentally. As for me, I have studied dressage primarily with Leslie Reid. I enjoy bringing young horses up the levels and have ridden through PSG/I1 on several horses."

Shaun Meyers is riding Fryso, an 11 year old Friesian gelding. From Shaun: "He and I have competed together through 2nd level. We are schooling 3rd and 4th level, with the exception of hitting a roadblock with the changes. We would like to work on continuing to improve our connection and anything that would help further our goal of the changes."

Taylor Harper is riding Dora, a 12 year old Hanoverian mare, just coming back from work after several years of being a broodmare. They are working towards competing at 3rd level this show season. Taylor has been involved with horses for 14+ years, dressage being her main focus for the past 7 years.
