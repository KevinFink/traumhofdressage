Clinics at Traumhof

Artwork by Jessica Lyman

STEFFEN PETERS CLINIC: May 14th and 15th, 2011

Although our primary focus here at Traumhof is ongoing training of our boarders and their horses by our in-house trainers, we also offer periodic clinics by outside experts. Our boarders get top priority for riding in or auditing these clinics, but we do invite other trainers to apply for riding slots, and often open a limited number of auditing seats to the dressage community at large.

In addition to great learning experiences, we try to make our clinics fun - we usually have a party afterwards so everyone can get to know the clinician and the other participants in a more casual atmosphere.

STEFFEN PETERS CLINIC: May 14th and 15th, 2011
Steffen Peters

We are pleased to welcome Steffen Peters to Traumhof for a clinic on May 14th and 15th. We have plenty of space for auditors and it should be an excellent learning opportunity to watch Steffen teach some of the best trainers in the Pacific Northwest! Click here to sign up.

Just in case you've been living under a (figurative) rock for the past 15 years, Steffen is a 3-time Olympian with multiple medals, won the FEI Rolex World Cup in 2009, swept the Grand Prix, the Special, and the Freestyle at Aachen (the first and only time an American rider has done this), won the World Dressage Masters Palm Beach CDI 5* in March, and swept the "small tour" (with a record score) at the Festival of the Horse CDI-W in early April. His intuitive horsemanship brings out the best in his equine partners and makes him a pleasure to watch and learn from.

Steffen has said, "I've spent most of my riding career bringing four and five year-old young horses through the levels to Grand Prix. To me there is no greater reward than spending six or seven years with a horse until they're confirmed at the highest level, and can go out and 'strut their stuff' with the best of them."

You can find out more about Steffen at any number of sites (including his own: SPeters Dressage), but this Video Biography of Steffen Peters will give you a good feel for his friendly personality and genuine love of horses.

Please join us for this educational and fun weekend!
