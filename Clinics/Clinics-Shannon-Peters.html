Clinics at Traumhof

Artwork by Jessica Lyman

Although our primary focus here at Traumhof is ongoing training of our boarders and their horses by our in-house trainers, we also offer occasional clinics by outside experts. Our boarders get top priority for riding in or auditing these clinics, but we do invite other trainers to apply for riding slots, and often open a limited number of auditing seats to the dressage community at large.

In addition to great learning experiences, we try to make our clinics fun - we usually have a party afterwards so everyone can get to know the clinician and the other participants in a more casual atmosphere. It typically involves food, wine, s'mores, music, and sometimes even moonwalking!

SHANNON PETERS CLINIC: November 13th and 14th, 2010
Shannon Peters

We are pleased to welcome Shannon Peters of SPeters Dressage back to Traumhof for a clinic on November 13th and 14th. We have plenty of space for auditors and it will be another excellent learning opportunity to watch Shannon teach some of the best riders in the Pacific Northwest! We will be charging $35 per day for auditing. Click here to sign up.

Please join us for this educational and fun weekend! Having Shannon up here is going to be a rare treat, as she does a limited number of clinics.

We will have a BBQ party on the patio Saturday evening, all welcome. Please RSVP on the auditing registration form so we have enough food and wine.
