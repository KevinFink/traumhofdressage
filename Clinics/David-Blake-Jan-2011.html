Horse & Rider Biographies

Susan Gilbert is riding Mambo, a 14 yo warmblood gelding. They are currently schooling Training level and moving into First. They are working on the basics - not spooking or pulling, moving forward, paying attention to the rider, etc. Susan notes that she is one of the few who learned to ride as an adult, so has only been riding for the last eight years. Mambo used to be an eventer and has trained as far as Fourth level in the past, so Susan is looking forward to learning from him as they progress up the levels! Susan and Mambo train with Erin Morris at Traumhof.

Danielle Odom is riding Nick, a 12 yo Welsh Cob / Thoroughbred cross. Danielle and Nick have been a team for five years. They competed at eventing for three years and competed at training and first level dressage last summer. Their project for this winter is relaxation! Danielle and Nick train with Nicki Grandia at Traumhof.

Nicki Grandia is riding Rudeau, Elisienne's son by Ruffian (x Rubenstein). Rudi competed First level this year, including winning the Oregon Dressage Society's First Level Championship. He has been schooling Second level and is just starting Third. His biggest limitations are tension in the body and focus in the brain. Nicki is a USDF Bronze, Silver, and Gold Medalist trainer at Traumhof.

Jillian Santi is riding Starwars TOF (Star), a 13 yo 14.1 hand Haflinger that she purchased when he was four. Jillian has personally trained Star to the FEI level and successfully competed at I-2 last year. Star was the first Haflinger to ever compete at an FEI level in the US and is believed to currently be the only Haflinger in the world to compete at I-2! Jillian hopes to show Grand Prix this year. Jillian and Star train with Nicki Grandia at Traumhof.

Linda Grandia is riding Eloge II. Elo is a 19 yo Swedish Warmblood. He has trained and shown in the Northwest all of his life. Elo and Linda are currently showing 4th level and hope to move up to Prix St George this summer. Linda Grandia teaches and trains at Hilltop Stables in Woodinville and is the proud mother of 3 equestrian children, Annie, Mark and Nicki Grandia.

Erin Morris is riding St. Anton, a 10 yo Finnish Warmblood gelding. Erin imported him from Germany as a 5 year old and has trained him up through Prix St George. The pair recently returned from a month training with Shannon Peters at Arroyo Del Mar. Erin is a USDF Bronze & Silver Medalist trainer at Traumhof.

Siggi Wolff is riding French Kiss (Frenchi), a seven yo Westfalian by Florestan I. Her goals for the clinic are suppleness, overall submission, quicker responds to aids, and help on his changes by finding the best exercises to set him up. His primary challenge is to get quicker behind in the collected gaits. Siggi comments "Frenchi has come a long ways on the submission front but still has occasional outbursts :)"

Alexander Holland is riding Wieneke Fan De Skarwei (Wieneke), a 11 yo Friesian mare. His goal for the clinic is to work on developing lateral bend and collection, learn exercises for submission, and quicken response to aids. Alex adds "Wieneke is very willing to learn and intelligent. She sometimes has a mind of her own and bullies through aids. I would like to show her this season at first and possibly second level. She is 9 months into recovery from a prior suspensory injury." A big shout-out to Alex for winning the Devonwood Sportmanship award at Dressage at DevonWood last summer! Alex trains with Siggi Wolff.
