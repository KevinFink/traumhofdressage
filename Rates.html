Traumhof Board and Training Rates

Artwork by Jessica Lyman

Board rates:

  • 12 x 12 stall: $655/mo
  • 12.5 x 12.5 stall: $690/mo
  • 12.5 x 15.5 stall: $740/mo
  • 12.5 x 16.5 stall: $750/mo

Board includes guarantee of 1/2 day turnout 7 days a week weather permitting. Feeding of Premium Timothy 5x/day (first feeding 6 am, last feeding 9 pm). Night check before bedtime with final hay feeding of the day. Grain choices include: Timothy pellets, Oats, Barley, Black Oil Sunflower Seeds (for Omegas), and Dynamite's PGR (complete feed). We also offer the Dynamite free choice NTM salt and 2:1 in each horse's stall. Jessica is a Dynamite distributor and can customize a feed program to your horse's individual needs if you desire. Blanketing, boots, Vet/Farrier holding/Scheduling, Trailer Parking, Handwalking. Warm (in the fall/winter/spring) filtered water. Auditing for all of our clinics. All of this is included in board. We are a full service barn. As an aside, but an important detail, we have our huge arena (80' x 204') sand/rubber footing, professionally leveled and "fluffed" every 4-6 months so that the footing is always as perfect as it can possibly be.

Training options:

Our fabulous Dressage trainer is Nicki Grandia, a USDF Bronze, Silver, Gold medalist. Her training rates are:
  • $700/month (full training)
  • $575/3 days a week
  • $55/45 min lesson

In addition to everything listed above, Nicki will be taking horses and clients to the local (WA and OR) Region 6 shows. Nicki is available to help you and your horse reach your riding goals whatever those may be.

Additional conditioning/Theraputic/Rehab options for your horse:

  • Theraplate: $175/month, guaranteed 5 days a week 2-3 times a day.
  • Treadmill (w/incline): $150/month, guaranteed 5 days a week.

Clinics:

As a Traumhof client you will have the option to ride with some of the top trainers in the country. We have hosted in the past Debbie McDonald, Steffen Peters, Shannon Peters, David Blake and we currently have a clinic series running with the fabulous Lientje Scheuler of SPeters Dressage. Lientje is the assistant trainer to Shannon and Steffen at their barn Arroyo Del Mar in San Diego, CA. Lientje trains with the horse first approach that the Peters use, and is a dynamic clinician, not to mention a well accomplished rider herself! This is a unique opportunity to train regularly with the best of the best! In between clinics, Nicki can keep you going with the same training style. Its a win-win for both you and your horse! Please see our clinic page for the current schedule and to register to audit.
