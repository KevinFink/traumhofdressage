Clinics at Traumhof

Artwork by Jessica Lyman

Although our primary focus here at Traumhof is ongoing training of our boarders and their horses by our in-house trainers, we also offer regular clinics by outside experts. Our boarders get top priority for riding in (and always get free auditing of) these clinics, but we do invite other riders to apply for riding slots, and always welcome auditors from the dressage community at large.

In addition to great learning experiences, we try to make our clinics fun - we usually have a dinner or party afterwards so everyone can get to know the clinician and the other participants in a more casual atmosphere.

Lientje Scheuler Clinic: May 16th & 17th, 2015

Lientje and Bugs

The Traumhof Clinic Series continues on the weekend of May 16th with Lientje Schueler, Assistant Trainer at S. Peters Dressage. Lientje started riding as a child in her home town of Oldenburg, Germany, started dressage at age 12, and has been riding and training ever since. She has been the Assistant Trainer for Shannon and Steffen for the past 8 years. Her website, TrainAndCompete.com, has more information including pictures and videos of her riding.

We have plenty of space for auditors and it should be an excellent learning opportunity to watch Lientje teach some of the best riders in the Pacific Northwest! We will be charging $35 per day (or $60 for the weekend if you sign up and pay ahead of time) for auditing. Click here to sign up.

Please join us for this educational and fun weekend!
