Clinics at Traumhof

Artwork by Jessica Lyman

Although our primary focus here at Traumhof is ongoing training of our boarders and their horses by our in-house trainers, we also offer regular clinics by outside experts. Our boarders get top priority for riding in (and always get free auditing of) these clinics, but we do invite other riders to apply for riding slots, and always welcome auditors from the dressage community at large.

In addition to great learning experiences, we try to make our clinics fun - we usually have a dinner or party afterwards so everyone can get to know the clinician and the other participants in a more casual atmosphere.
